**# Marketing Funnel \& Conversion Performance Analysis**



This project analyzes marketing funnel performance to identify conversion drop-offs and optimize lead-to-customer conversion.



**## Funnel Stages**


Visitors → Leads → Qualified Leads → Customers



**## Key Insights**


\- Organic search generates the highest number of customers.

\- Email marketing shows strong funnel conversion efficiency.

\- LinkedIn ads produce high-quality leads.

\- Facebook ads show lower conversion performance.



**## Tools Used**


\- Excel

\- Data Visualization

\- Marketing Funnel Analytics



**## Business Recommendations**


\- Invest more in SEO and organic acquisition.

\- Expand email marketing campaigns.

\- Improve Facebook ad targeting.

\- Use LinkedIn ads for high-intent audiences.
